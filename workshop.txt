description=[h1]About Inventory Tetris[/h1]
description=Inventory Tetris is a comprehensive inventory overhaul for Project Zomboid by [b]notloc[/b].
description=[list]
description=[*] The inventory is replaced with a grid based system.
description=[*] All items now have a size.
description=[*] [b]See inside all your equipped bags at once[/b]
description=[*] Bags now provide additional inventory space, not just weight reduction.
description=[*] Worn items do not take up inventory space.
description=[*] No more shoving 3 boxes of cereal and a carton of milk in your pants.
description=[/list]

description=[h1]FAQ[/h1]
description=[h2]How do I open the keyring?[/h2]
description=Double click it. The items will be shown in a pop-up window.
description=
description=[h2]Furniture is too big for my bag![/h2]
description=Use your hands. Equip the furniture as you would a weapon.
description=Your hands can only hold 1 item each, but the items they hold can be as big as you want.
description=
description=[h2]How do I place a thing? It is too big for my main inventory.[/h2]
description=If you want to place a large item such as a tent or furniture, equip the item into your hand as if it was a weapon.
description=Once the item is equipped, it will appear as an option when trying to place/build objects.
description=
description=[h2]My game lags / Low FPS[/h2]
description=Ensure that the following game options is CHECKED:
description=[code]Options -> Display -> UI -> UI Offscreen Rendering -> CHECK[/code]
description=You can now use the use the UI Rendering FPS dropdown to choose a target FPS to reduce the performance demand.
description=
description=[h2]Can I add/remove this on existing saves?[/h2]
description=Yes.
description=Inventory Tetris can added/removed at any point without issue.
description=(Note: When adding the mod the player may drop items to the ground if they do not have enough space.)
